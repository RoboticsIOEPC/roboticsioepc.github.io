An intensive 8-day training program by the Robotics Club in collaboration with FSU and in association with  ACES, EXCESS, SOMES, ELESS, and NAESS.  Designed for students eager to explore the world of robotics, this program offers a perfect blend of foundational concepts and hands-on experience. 
The bootcamp covered :
- Foundation of Robotics
- Introduction to Microcontrollers
- Sensors & Applications
- Wired Communication and Actuators
- Power Management Systems
- Wireless Communication Techniques
- Product Design and 3d Printing
- Project Implementation and DOcumentation

We want to extend a sincere and heartfelt gratitude to each of our amazing tutors, volunteers, and the incredible organizing team for the unwavering support and success of the session. Here some glimpses of the happy and content hearts.
